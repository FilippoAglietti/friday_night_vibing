## Description

### The Problem
Creating an online course used to take weeks. You'd spend days outlining, scripting, recording, editing — and the result often looked generic. Meanwhile, your expertise sits unused because the production barrier is too high.

### What Syllabi Does
Syllabi is an AI-powered course generator that turns any topic into a polished, professional curriculum in 60 seconds.

**Here's what you get:**
- **Complete course structure** — Modules, lessons, learning objectives, and pacing schedules
- **AI-generated quizzes** — Multiple choice, true/false, and short answer with explanations
- **Audio narration** — Professional AI voice narration for every lesson (Pro Max)
- **Rich lesson content** — Full markdown content with key points, examples, and resources
- **Export everywhere** — PDF, DOCX, PPTX, Notion blocks, Markdown, and shareable web links
- **16 languages** — Generate courses natively in English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, and 11 more
- **Lead magnet mode** — Gate courses behind email capture and use them to grow your list
- **Beautiful share links** — Every course gets a branded shareable page, no hosting needed

### Who It's For
- **Course creators** who want to ship faster and stop procrastinating on production
- **Coaches & consultants** who need structured training materials for clients
- **Corporate trainers** building onboarding, sales training, or compliance courses
- **Teachers & professors** who want polished supplementary materials
- **Marketers** using mini-courses as lead magnets to grow email lists

### How It Works
1. Enter your topic and choose your style (difficulty, length, teaching approach)
2. AI generates a complete curriculum in ~60 seconds
3. Edit, customize, then export or share with a single click

### What Makes Syllabi Different
- **Audio narration** — We're the only AI course generator with built-in voice narration
- **Export versatility** — PDF, DOCX, PPTX, Notion, Markdown, share links with email gating
- **Multilingual** — 16 languages, not just translation — native generation
- **Beautiful output** — Courses look designed, not AI-generated
- **Free tier** — 3 free generations, no credit card required

## Maker Story

> Here's the dirty secret of online courses: **most learners never finish them.** Industry average completion rate is around 5-15%. Your course can be brilliant — if nobody gets past module two, none of that matters.
>
> My co-founder and I spent years watching this happen. Experts pouring weeks into courses that looked like a Google Doc, sounded like a textbook, and got abandoned by the third lesson. Meanwhile, the same people would finish a 10-hour podcast in a week.
>
> So we asked a different question. Not "how do we help people **create** courses faster?" — that's a crowded race. We asked "how do we help creators make courses their students **actually finish?**"
>
> That's what Syllabi is. Every course is audio-narrated so learners can listen on commutes, walks, or at the gym. Every course is visually designed so it doesn't feel like a wall of text. Every course has real pacing, not just a module dump. And yes, every course takes 60 seconds to generate.
>
> We're not the fastest AI course generator. We're the one that actually gets finished.
>
> — Gianmarco & Filippo, co-founders
