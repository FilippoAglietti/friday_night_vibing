## The Formula

A great lead magnet course follows this structure:

1. **Quick Win** — Give them an immediate result in lesson 1
2. **Framework** — Teach your signature method in lessons 2-3
3. **Gap** — Show what's possible with deeper work (lesson 4-5)
4. **Bridge** — Connect to your paid offer naturally

## Optimization Notes

After running for 2 weeks, review:

1. **Where do people drop off?** (Check email open rates per lesson)
2. **What gets the most replies/engagement?** (Double down on that)
3. **Is the CTA converting?** (If not, adjust lesson 5 or add a limited-time offer)
